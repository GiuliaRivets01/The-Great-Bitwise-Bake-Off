Preheat the oven at 190 degrees.
Mix together banana, flour, cream cheese, butter, white sugar, butter, and unsalted butter in a bowl.
In another bowl, beat together lemon juice, baking soda, egg, baking soda, salt, baking soda, and kosher salt.
Chill the cookie dough in the refrigerator for 3 hour.
Now roll the cookie dough into balls and place them on a baking tray.
Bake for 35 minutes.
